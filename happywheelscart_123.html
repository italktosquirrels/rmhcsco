What is The Happy Wheels Cart?

The real-life Happy Wheels Cart is filled with snacks, drinks, toys, and toiletries. RMHCSCO volunteers take the cart around the hospital to the families unable to leave their child's bedside. It offers parents a moment of peace and eases the stress of their long days while keeping them close to their little ones. Because of your generous donations, this in-hospital program continues to reach families beyond the walls of our home.
