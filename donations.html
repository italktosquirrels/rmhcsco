Make A Donation

Thank you for supporting Ronald McDonald House Charities® South Central Ontario (RMHC®SCO) and the families we serve. Please select the type of gift you would like to make below.

Happy Wheels Cart

The Happy Wheels Cart, equipped with snacks, drinks, toys and activities, travels from room to room to provide a much-needed break for parents and children unable to leave their hospital beds. Make a small donation and watch the virtual Happy Wheels Cart travel around Hamilton, filling families with hope along the way. 

Find Out More!

Individual/General Donation

When you make a monetary donation you are giving families the ability to stay close to their children while they are receiving medical treatment at local area hospitals. 

Donate

In HONOUR/In Memory Donation

A donation made in honour of a wedding, birthday, anniversary, or just because, provides an opportunity to celebrate family and friends in a meaningful and impactful way. A donation made in memory of a loved one is a beautiful personal expression. Memorial donations inspire and enable RMHCSCO to continue delivering our mission. 

Donate

Corporate/Business Donation

When your company chooses us as their "Charity of Choice" for workplace fundraisers, employee giving/corporate matching programs, or for any other reason, you can feel proud knowing your business is helping families stay close when it matters most.

Donate

Fundraiser/Community Event Donation

When you make a donation from proceeds raised at a fundraising event, you are supporting our mission while raising community awareness. Bake sales, class fundraisers, and golf tournaments can make an incredible difference for our families.

Make Fun Matter

Become A Monthly donor

When you become a monthly donor, your donation builds a foundation of ongoing and reliable funding for RMHCSCO. No matter the amount, your monthly donation will help to maintain our House and Family Rooms, stock our cupboards and keep our bedrooms comfortable for families. The convenience of a recurring donation allows for continuous support through automatic billing which allows sick children to have what is needed the most... their families.
